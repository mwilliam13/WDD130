Overview

Purpose

Dry Oar is a family-friendly, excitement-ready, adventure-driven, saftey-first organization of rafting experts prepared to take our clients' skills to the next feasible level on the water. We enjoy pushing the comfort level to adventureous and exciting, yet staying within the confines of saftey for all involved. When mistakes happen, and they do, we have the right staff trained to understand and know the next step to bring things to proper order.

The purpose of the Dry Oar website is to inform and communicate the service offerings we have for our clients. It will expand on the services, schedules, availability, expectations, saftey, and other pertinent information we feel is most valuable and requested for clients both new and returning. As a public portal into our business, the content will help drive interest into our vertical and our company, which will increase revenue. As we prepare and diseminate information, both expectations and educational, it will keep our clients informed on saftey and understanding of how we operate. After experiences with our clients, we can post images and testimonials of the fun, excitement, and team-building opportunities they have had, thus improving interest and revenues.

Audience

If you are ready for an exciting, fun-filled experience where you don't care what is on TV anymore, then you are who we want to continue reading. If you interact with your computer only long enough to read this site, get excited, jump into your car, navigate with your smart phone with our instruction to pound on our front door to get into a raft, then you are our client! If you aren't scared to push your comfort level, see the line that makes you excited for more, and explore the development of your skill, then you are in the right place! The coloration, typography, flow, and other visual elements will be prepared to improve navigability, invite interest, and distil the most important information where it is expected and needed the most.
