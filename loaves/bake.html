Home Prep Starter Flour Proof Bake

Here my loaf is pictured in an upside-down lid for a Lodge Dutch Oven. I prefer these Dutch Ovens to a pricey Bread Cloche, at 1/3 of most options. Plus it is versitle for other applications outside of baking. Trapped steam from the loaf circulates just fine for the raise portion of the bake and diminishes about the right time for the Maillard reaction to nicely carmelize the edges of the loaf.
