Starter

There are health benefits to eating home-baked sourdough. The leavening agent in sourdough is a SCOBY (Symbiotic Culture of Bacteria and Yeast), like what's found in yogurt. These beneficial microbes play a crucial role in gut health, aiding digestion and potentially boosting the immune system. Sourdough fermentation also breaks down gluten and antinutrients, making the bread easier to digest and the nutrients more accessible.
